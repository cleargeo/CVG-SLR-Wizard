Sea Level Rise Projection Wizard

Station-specific NOAA TR-083 relative sea level rise projections for coastal resilience planning

NOAA Sweet et al. 2022 14 Tide Gauge Stations 6 IPCC Scenarios 2020 – 2100 Horizon

📍  Step 1 — Tide Gauge Station

Select the NOAA CO-OPS station nearest your project site. Station selection controls the local vertical land motion (VLM) correction applied to RSLR projections per NOAA TR-083 (Sweet et al. 2022).

📊  Step 2 — NOAA Scenario

Select the SLR planning scenario from NOAA TR-083 (Sweet et al. 2022). The Intermediate scenario is the FEMA-recommended baseline for coastal planning and the Florida BRACE standard.

📅  Step 3 — Planning Horizon

Select the target year for your sea level rise projection (2020–2100). Values between tabular intervals are linearly interpolated per NOAA TR-083 Appendix C.

20202040206020802100
2070
ℹ️ Regulatory guidance: For FEMA Resilience Action Plan compliance, a 50-year horizon (2070) is standard. For critical infrastructure and transportation assets, use 2100 (100-year horizon). The Intermediate scenario at 2070 is required under Florida's BRACE resilience guidance.
